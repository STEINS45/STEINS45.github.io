About me

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Computer Science, starting in Fall 2025. Prior to this, I was a Research Associate at the School of Information Sciences, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I also closely collaborated with Zeyi Huang at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Prior to UIUC, I obtained my M.S. degree from New York University and B.E. degree from Southwest Jiaotong University.

My research interest is computer vision.
